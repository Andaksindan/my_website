Dead Space 3: Terror in the Tundra

Gaming · Survival Horror

Dead Space 3 often gets a bad wrap for leaning into action, but revisited today, it remains a fascinating and highly polished experience.

The Frozen Desert

Tau Volantis provides a stark contrast to the cramped corridors of the Ishimura. The sense of isolation in a vast, white wasteland is palpable.

The Weapon Bench

The crafting system in DS3 was ahead of its time. The level of customization allowed players to truly build tools of survival that fit their playstyle.

Cosmic Horror at Scale

While it lost some of the intimate horror of the first two, the scale of the threat in Dead Space 3 — the Brother Moons — remains one of the most chilling concepts in sci-fi horror.
