Black Myth: Wukong – A New Legend

Gaming · Action RPG

Black Myth: Wukong is more than just a visually stunning game; it's a deep dive into one of literature's most enduring figures.

Visual Splendor

The attention to detail in the character designs and environments is breathtaking. It's a benchmark for what modern hardware can achieve.

Combat with Character

The transformation system and the variety of stances make combat feel dynamic and true to the Monkey King's legendary abilities.

A Story for the Ages

By leaning heavily into the source material, Journey to the West, Game Science has created a world that feels ancient, mystical, and completely fresh to global audiences.
