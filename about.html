Meet the Creator

Piyush Gedam is a Python Developer, author, and technologist based in Nagpur, Maharashtra.

By day, he works in a multinational corporation. by night, he builds digital worlds. A visionary with a passion for coding, gaming, and travel, Piyush created Now & Noticed to share his obsession with the things that make life interesting.

This website is currently under active development.

Our Mission

At Now & Noticed, we're dedicated to exploring and sharing insights on a diverse range of topics, from the intricate mechanics of watches to the captivating narratives of anime, the exhilarating world of cars, and the profound impact of lifestyle choices.

Our mission is to provide engaging, well-researched, and thought-provoking content that not only informs but also inspires our readers to discover new passions and perspectives. We believe that every subject, no matter how niche, holds a story worth telling.

What We Cover

Our content spans several key categories:

  • Watches: Deep dives into horology, reviews of classic and contemporary timepieces, and explorations of watchmaking heritage.
  • Anime: Analyses of beloved series, recommendations, and discussions on the cultural impact and artistic merit of Japanese animation.
  • Cars: From historical retrospectives to reviews of modern marvels, we cover the engineering, design, and culture surrounding automobiles.
  • Lifestyle: Thought pieces on minimalism, productivity, urban living, and trends that shape our daily lives.
  • Movies: Reviews, analyses, and recommendations of films across genres, exploring their themes, direction, and performances.
  • Sports: Celebrating iconic moments, legendary athletes, and the strategies that define the world of sports.
  • Trending: Keeping an eye on the latest cultural phenomena, technological advancements, and societal shifts.

Our Philosophy

We believe in quality over quantity, delivering content that is both insightful and enjoyable to read. Our team of passionate writers and researchers strives to bring you fresh perspectives and comprehensive coverage on topics that truly matter.

Thank you for joining us on this journey of discovery!
