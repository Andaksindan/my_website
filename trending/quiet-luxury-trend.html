Quiet Luxury: The New Status Symbol

Trending · Lifestyle

No logos. No flash. Just obscenely expensive clothing that looks understated. Welcome to quiet luxury.

What Is Quiet Luxury?

It's Loro Piana cashmere, Hermès scarves without the H, and $800 plain white t-shirts. The point isn't to flex — it's to signal wealth to those who know.

Why Logomania Died

Logomania peaked and became gauche. Now the truly rich want to separate themselves from people with knockoffs. Quiet luxury is gatekeeping through knowledge.

The Role of Succession

The show popularized this aesthetic. Kendall Roy's Loro Piana baseball cap became a cultural touchstone. Everyone wanted to dress like a billionaire in crisis.

Final Thought

Quiet luxury isn't humble — it's the ultimate flex. It says: "I don't need to prove anything to you."
