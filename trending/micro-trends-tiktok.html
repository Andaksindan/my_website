The Rise of Micro-Trends

Trending · Culture

Trends used to last years. Now they last weeks. TikTok accelerated culture to an unsustainable speed.

What Are Micro-Trends?

Aesthetic movements that explode, saturate, and die within months. Think "clean girl aesthetic" or "blokecore." They rise fast and burn out faster.

TikTok's Role

The algorithm rewards novelty. Once something goes viral, everyone copies it. Then everyone gets bored. The cycle repeats endlessly.

The Environmental Cost

Fast fashion thrives on micro-trends. Shein produces new styles weekly to keep up. The waste is staggering.

The Fatigue Is Real

People are exhausted. Keeping up with trends is now a full-time job. The backlash is building toward timeless, slow fashion.

Final Thought

Micro-trends are unsustainable — culturally, environmentally, mentally. Something has to give.
