Dopamine Fasting: Pseudo-Science or Self-Care?

Trending · Wellness

Silicon Valley's latest trend: abstaining from pleasure to "reset" your brain. The science says otherwise.

What Is Dopamine Fasting?

Avoiding all pleasurable activities — social media, food, music, conversation — to supposedly reset dopamine receptors and increase motivation.

The Science Says No

Neuroscientists have debunked this. You can't "fast" from dopamine. It's a neurotransmitter your brain always produces. The concept is fundamentally wrong.

What People Are Actually Doing

Taking breaks from overstimulation. Which is good! But calling it "dopamine fasting" misrepresents the biology and sounds pretentious.

The Real Benefit

Digital detoxes work. Not because of dopamine, but because they give your attention a break. The branding is just Silicon Valley self-optimization theater.

Final Thought

Take breaks from your phone. Just don't pretend it's neuroscience.
