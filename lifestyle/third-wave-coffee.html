Coffee Culture and the Third Wave

Lifestyle · Food & Drink

Coffee went from commodity to craft. Now it's wine-level snobbery — and that's not entirely a bad thing.

The Three Waves

First wave: Folgers, Maxwell House. Coffee as fuel. Second wave: Starbucks, espresso drinks. Third wave: single-origin, pour-over, precision.

What Makes Third Wave Different

Direct trade with farmers. Light roasts that preserve flavor. Manual brewing. Every variable controlled. Coffee as art.

The Snobbery Problem

Baristas lecture you about tasting notes. $7 pour-overs. Gatekeeping. The culture can be alienating.

The Good Part

Farmers get paid fairly. Quality improved dramatically. People care about what they're drinking. That's progress.

Final Thought

Third-wave coffee elevated the craft. But don't let anyone shame you for liking what you like. Drink good coffee. Ignore the snobs.
