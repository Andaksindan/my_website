The 2008 Olympics: Usain Bolt's Coronation

Sports · Track & Field

Beijing 2008. Usain Bolt broke three world records — and made it look easy. Speed had a new definition.

The 100m: Showboating at 9.69

He slowed down. Celebrated before the finish. Still broke the world record. Imagine if he'd tried. That's the absurdity of Bolt.

The 200m: 19.30 Seconds

This one he ran properly. The result? An untouchable world record. No one has come close since.

The 4x100m Relay

Jamaica set a world record. Bolt anchored. Three golds. Three world records. One Olympics. Unprecedented dominance.

The Personality

Bolt wasn't just fast. He was charismatic, playful, magnetic. He turned track and field into appointment viewing.

Final Thought

Bolt didn't just dominate his sport. He transcended it. And 2008 was the moment the world realized they were watching someone impossible.
